## DESIGN QUALITY NOTES

- Every interactive element must have a clearly visible hover state
- Use 8px consistent spacing rhythm throughout
- All text must meet WCAG AA contrast on their respective backgrounds
- The design should feel "premium SaaS" — similar to Linear, Vercel, or Raycast in polish level
- Avoid generic dark-mode aesthetics — use subtle depth through layering (L1/L2/L3 surfaces) not shadows
- No decorative gradients except the avatar/bot icon circles
